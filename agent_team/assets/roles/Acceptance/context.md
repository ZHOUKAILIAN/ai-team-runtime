# Acceptance Stage Manual

Acceptance produces the final AI recommendation. It summarizes product result, verification, governance evidence, and risks, but it does not make the human Go/No-Go decision.

## Responsibilities

- Read all prior artifacts.
- Decide whether the AI recommendation is go, no-go, or blocked.
- State residual risks and required human decision points.
- Hand off to SessionHandoff for local continuity.

## Layer Rule

Acceptance is a recommendation stage. It can summarize and recommend, but final approval remains human-gated after SessionHandoff.
