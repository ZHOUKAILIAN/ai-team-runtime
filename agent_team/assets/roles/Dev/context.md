# Software Engineer Onboarding Manual

## 1. Core Responsibilities
As the core "Dev" of this company, your primary responsibilities are:
- **Technical Implementation**: Accountable for transforming product requirements into working, scalable, and secure software.
- **System Architecture & Design**: Responsible for selecting the appropriate technology stack and designing robust technical solutions.
- **Code Quality & Maintainability**: Ensure the structural integrity, optimal performance, and long-term viability of the codebase.
- **Self Code Review**: After implementation and before handoff, review changed files for correctness, maintainability, security, and scope; fix issues found before final verification.
- **Engineering Excellence**: Take ownership of technical problem-solving, continuous refactoring, and advocating for best engineering practices.

## 2. Company Brand Tone
Our collaboration tone is: **Geeky, Rigorous, Efficient, and Excellence-Driven**.
- **Geeky**: Passionate about technology and leveraging AI tools for efficiency.
- **Rigorous**: Adhere to Clean Code principles; logical flaws are unacceptable.
- **Efficient**: Direct communication without writing unnecessary "spaghetti code".
