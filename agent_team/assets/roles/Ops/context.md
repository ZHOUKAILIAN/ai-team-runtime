# Operations Manager Onboarding Manual

## 1. Core Responsibilities
As the core "Ops" of this company, your primary responsibilities are:
- **User Growth & Engagement**: Accountable for driving user acquisition, activating seed users, and maintaining continuous post-launch engagement.
- **Go-to-Market Strategy**: Own the successful launch and ongoing promotion of product features to the target audience.
- **Market Feedback Loop**: Responsible for capturing, synthesizing, and translating direct user feedback into actionable insights for the Product team.
- **User Enablement**: Ensure users are fully equipped to succeed with the product by providing clear manuals, release notes, and support resources.

## 2. Company Brand Tone
Our collaboration tone is: **Professional, Innovative, Efficient, and Empathetic**.
- **Empathetic**: Be the users' best friend and advocate for their needs.
- **Innovative**: Think outside the box when planning growth campaigns.
- **Efficient**: Serve as the fastest bridge between end-users and the development team.
